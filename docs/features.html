BTC Tracker Features

BTC Tracker is packed with powerful features designed to help you monitor and manage your Bitcoin investments. Here's a comprehensive overview of what you can do with our application.

Transaction Management

Add, edit, and delete Bitcoin transactions with support for multiple currencies. Track buys, sells, and other transaction types with detailed information.

Performance Tracking

Monitor your investment performance with profit/loss calculations and percentages. See how your Bitcoin holdings are performing at a glance.

Historical Data

View historical Bitcoin price data to analyze market trends. Track how your investments have performed over time and make informed decisions.

CSV Import/Export

Import transactions from CSV files or export your data for backup or analysis. Makes it easy to migrate from other tracking solutions or create backups.

Multi-currency Support

Support for multiple currencies with automatic exchange rate conversion. View your portfolio value in your preferred currency.

Secure Authentication

Protect your financial data with secure user authentication. Your investment information remains private and accessible only to you.

Self-Hosted

Run BTC Tracker on your own infrastructure for complete control and privacy. Your data never leaves your server and isn't shared with third parties.

Dark Mode

Built-in dark mode support for comfortable viewing in low-light environments. Easy on the eyes and looks great on any device.

Responsive Design

Access BTC Tracker from any device with a responsive design that adapts to your screen size. Works well on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Local Data Storage

All data is stored locally in JSON files for simplicity and portability. Easy to backup, move, or restore your transaction history.

Docker Support

Easy deployment using Docker and Docker Compose. Get up and running in minutes with minimal configuration.
