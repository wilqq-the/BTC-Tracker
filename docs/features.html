Powerful Features

BTC Tracker is packed with everything you need to monitor and manage your Bitcoin investments with complete privacy and control.

Transaction Management

Add, edit, and organize your Bitcoin transactions with support for multiple currencies and exchange integrations. Track buys, sells, and transfers with detailed information.

Multiple currencies Exchange integration Transaction categories

Performance Analytics

Track your investment performance with real-time P&L calculations, ROI analysis, and interactive charts. Monitor your portfolio's growth over time.

Real-time P&L ROI tracking Interactive charts

Display amounts in both BTC and Satoshis with the official symbol for precise tracking. Perfect for small transactions and DCA strategies.

Symbol Precise tracking DCA friendly

All data stays on your device. No external servers, no data sharing, complete privacy and control over your financial information.

Easy migration Data backup Analysis ready
